A small manifesto.

Three principles · no asterisks
i.

Coverage over fame.

We'd rather have one tool you needed at 2am than ten you'll never use. The library grows quietly — finance, health, regional units, the obscure ones nobody else built.

ii.

Search is the interface.

A developer and a designer need different AI tools, but they shop the same library. Six paths to browse — one search to pinpoint. Type what you mean. We aggregate, augment, present.

iii.

No tricks. Ever.

No popups. No autoplay. No signup wall. Affiliate links exist, they're labelled, they sit beside the result — never on top of it. You're a reader, not a product.
